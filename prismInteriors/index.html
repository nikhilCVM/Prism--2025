About us

The Prism Interiors – A Freelance Studio

Founded in 2022, The Prism Interiors was born from a simple idea — to bring clarity, fairness, and quality to an interior design market that often lacks all three. After observing how clients are misled by fake promises, inflated quotes, poor materials, and unkept warranties, we decided to be different. We operate as a transparent, freelance design studio, offering stylish, functional interiors without hidden costs or fake discounts. We don’t upsell. We don’t subcontract blindly. We work directly with verified vendors and offer clear pricing upfront, so you know exactly where your money is going. Whether you want to design a full home or just a single wall, we treat every project with equal care. You get:

⚫ Honest design consultation
⚫ Transparent vendor pricing
⚫ Budget-conscious options without cutting quality
⚫ No fake warranties or material compromises
⚫ Personalized service from a small, dedicated team

At The Prism Interiors, we’re not here to play the “margin game.” We’re here to earn your trust, design your space with integrity, and prove that good interiors don’t need to be complicated or costly.
